Movie Review

Rating: 4.5/5.0

Movie Name: Deadpool X Wolverin

Release Date: July 26, 2024

Starring: Rayan Reynolds, Leslie Uggams, Chris Hemsworth Hugh jackman, Blake Lively, Emma corrin, Dafne keen Jennifer Garner, Morena Baccarin, Matthev macfadyen, Brianna hildebrand, Wesley Snipes, Tyler Mane and others.

Director: Shawn Levy

Producer: Simon Kinberg, MARVEL STUDIOS

Music Director: Rob simonson

Cinematographer: George Richmond

Editor: Shane Reid, Dean Zimmerman

Plus Points: It's a very good action packed fun film. The breaking the Fox jokes and speaking to the camera jokes are too funny. I can definitely see more sequels for these two on the horizon.

Minus Points: The story is really forgettable as a whole and is doesn't have the urgency to it that it should have had. Also as epic as the final act battle was, there wasn't really any development behind it and is just there to give Deadpool and Wolverine one final fight
